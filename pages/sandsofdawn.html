Sands of Dawn

Sands of Dawn is my first soulslike game and my initial project in Unreal Engine. As a gameplay developer, I implemented the gameplay mechanics and animations, managing the integration between C++ and Blueprints. I focused on creating a fluid and engaging experience, ensuring that the combat and movement dynamics were balanced and precise, capturing the essence and challenge typical of the soulslike genre.
