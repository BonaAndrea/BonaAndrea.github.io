Pandemood

During the Game Design course at PoliTo, I was part of a 6-person team that developed Pandemood, a puzzle-platformer set in a post-Covid world. The game aims to help players understand the importance of emotions. As the gameplay developer, I worked on the implementation of the gameplay mechanics, ensured a fun and precise experience, while also working on the UI, with a focus on the dialogue system.
