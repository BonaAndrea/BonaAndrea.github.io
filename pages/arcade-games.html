Arcade games

During my time as Unity Developer at TGE Ventures SRL, I worked on a number of simple arcade games. My tasks on these games were to design the game experience, implement it and ensure its quality; as well as implementing the UI, sounds and just about everything else that enriched the game experience. Some of the games I worked on include sidescroller platformers, three-match games, bubble shooter puzzle games, and more.
