Alpine Rescue Simulator

As part of the Virtual Reality course at PoliTo, I developed Alpine Rescue Simulator, a VR project using Unity. The game features three unique mountain rescue scenarios, including avalanche and canine rescue. I was tasked with designing the game experience, coming up with the ideas for the scenarios after talking to alpine rescue experts. I also focused on the user experience, designing the interaction with the game. Moreover, I worked on the actual gameplay implementation, ensuring a smooth and fun gameplay and briefly touching upon some basic AI implementation.
