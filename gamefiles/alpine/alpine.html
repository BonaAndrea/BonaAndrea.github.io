Alpine Rescue 2.0

In an effort to keep my projects alive and up to date, I'm working on a remake of the Alpine Rescue project. I'm focusing on updating the visual effects, working on better implementation of the gameplay mechanics while preserving the original feeling of the game. I'm also improving heavily on the optimization of the app. The project is currently ongoing and in progress.

Please note: the demo presented here had to be scaled down in order to work with WebGL. Therefore, bugs and grafical glitches might appear. It is strongly advised to play the full version of the game.
